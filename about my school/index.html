Playpen

Playpen is a playgroup to grade 12 English-medium school in Bashundhara Residential Area, Dhaka, Bangladesh that was established in 1977 by Zeba khan. It follows the curriculum of Cambridge International Examination (O-level) and Advanced Level (A-level) examinations.he school has a handball,football and basketball teams. It also has a debate, baking, table tennis and drama clubs.The school respectivly hosts several competitions among other schools.
